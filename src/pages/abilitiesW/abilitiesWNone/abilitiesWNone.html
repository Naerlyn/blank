Using W alone.

Here are the bugs that W has alone.

1) The next basic attack used after the 2nd cast of W can (not so unfrequently) deal no damage.

2) Mordekaiser can heal an enemy Braum if he blocks the heal particles.

3) This spell can cause FPS issues to any player in the game.

4) This spell will never grant you an assist unless they actually damage the slain enemy.

5) If Mordekaiser casts his W when he's alone, if a minion then enters the binding range, nothing will happen.

6) Mordekaiser can stop trying to attack enemies when his W pops at the end of the 4 seconds of the effect.

7) This spell can be heard by enemies through fog of war.
